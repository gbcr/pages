#WaveItForward and win Apple App Store giftcards!

Oct 10, 2014

There’s no denying a wave is the simplest and easiest wave to communicate. In a split second you can instantly put a smile on someone’s face, or even reconnect with those who you’ve perhaps drifted out of contact with…  

That’s why here at team wave, we’ve giving away $200 of Apple App Store giftcards to the best and most shared original wave photos.   

The person who posts the best waving photo will receive $200 to spend in the Apple App Store, two second prize winners will receive $100 each and three third prize winners will receive $50 each.  

Sound good to you? Ok, here’s how you participate:   

  1. Post your best waving photo to Facebook or Twitter, using hashtag #WaveitForward and tag @WaveitForward (otherwise we can’t find you!)
  2. Like us on Facebook or follow us on Twitter.
  3. Get your friends to share the photo to increase your chances of winning!

    4. Good luck to all those involved! Make sure you like us on Facebook and Twitter to be kept up to date with the leading waves! We’ll like or retweet to show you we’ve received your entry. Competition closes 26th Oct, 2014.
