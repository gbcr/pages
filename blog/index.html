#WaveItForward and earn money for your favourite charity!

Oct 1, 2014

There’s no denying a wave is the simplest and easiest wave to communicate. In a split second you can instantly put a smile on someone’s face, or even reconnect with those you’ve perhaps drifted out of contact with.  

In fact, waving is a lot like giving to charity, in that you through giving a little you make a big difference to those you most care about.

That’s why here at team wave, we’ve decided to give $1 per wave to the charity which receives the most waves this month.  

Yup, you read that correctly. The charity who has the most wavers behind them after four weeks will receive a dollar per wave, it’s as simple as that. Just think about the difference that money could make to your favourite charity.   

And the best thing is it requires little on your behalf.  

  1. Take a wave of yourself or someone else. Please note, it has to be an original wave photo.
  2. Write a post on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram, and tag at least two friends who you nominate to wave. 
  3. Choose your charity, and if possible use their social network @ ID .
  4. Most importantly, use hashtag #WaveitForward, otherwise we can’t find your entry!  

There, told you it was simple! Here’s a sample tweet.

Good luck to all those involved! Make sure you like us on Facebook and Twitter to be kept up to date with the leading waves!  Oh, and please no multiple waves. Strictly one wave per person across all media channels. If you want your charity to win, just encourage your friends to wave!
